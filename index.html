Our vision

Our vision is to work in partnership with our pupils, parents, and the community at large to create a positive and inclusive environment for high quality learning and teaching where everyone is valued and respected.

Students at Trinity are motivated to develop leadership skills and a thorough understanding of interdependence. Special emphasis is placed on the need to serve others while actively contributing to the school community and society as a whole. In the past twenty years our strength has doubled and this enables us to know that we are empowered by the unwavering trust that parents have in this institution, and in this knowledge lies our strength and inspiration.
